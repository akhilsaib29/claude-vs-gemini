Mobile & Remote Access

Access from Anywhere — What's Actually True

Verified facts about mobile availability and what happens when your device is off.

💡
On "Runs When Device Is Off" — The Truth for Both Platforms

Both Claude for Work and Gemini Enterprise are cloud-based. All AI processing runs on Anthropic's / Google's servers — never on your device. This means tasks you start (research, agentic runs, long analyses) continue server-side even if you close your browser, lock your phone, or shut your laptop. You get a push notification when done. This is included in the standard plan for both platforms. No special tier needed.

Recommendation: Claude for Work

For a credit-rating firm with stringent data governance, complex analytical workloads, and a need to scale BD without losing personalisation — Claude for Work is the superior platform. It wins on reasoning depth, instruction precision, regulatory compliance posture, Projects for institutional memory, local file access, MCP-native CRM integration, pricing flexibility, and mobile accessibility out of the box. The only area where Gemini holds an edge is raw context window size (2M vs 200K) and native Google Workspace embedding for Docs/Sheets/Gmail workflows.
